About T.K MOTORS

This company was built by the late grand parents in 2015. It aimed to satisfy its customers at all costs.

In T.K MOTORS, we design and built best cars, we built both gasoline and electric cars in both manual and automatic

transmition, we also buy used cars and resell them. In 2018, the company decided that it is best if it had cars for hire

for people who can not affort to buy their own cars
